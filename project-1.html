Form Buddy

Form Buddy is an online on-duty form submission platform designed to streamline the process of submitting on-duty requests for students.

Live Link

Project Overview

Form Buddy simplifies the process of applying for on-duty requests by digitizing the workflow. Students can submit their requests directly to concerned faculties, ensuring a seamless experience.

Built with modern web technologies, Form Buddy enhances user experience and minimizes manual errors, making the approval process faster and more efficient.
